About This Dashboard

A personal command centre built by Abdulwajid (Jid) — data analyst, journalism, canva designer, junior developer, Man City supporter and an all-round great guy based in Nigeria. Everything on this page is live, interactive and built from scratch.

💱
Naira Exchange Rates
Live USD, GBP and EUR rates fetched from open.er-api.com and cached hourly. Reflects real market rates, not bank rates.
📈
Market Watch
Live crypto prices for BTC, ETH, SOL — and the Man City fan token (CITY). Updates every 60 seconds via CoinGecko.
🌤
Nigeria Weather
Real-time temperature and conditions for Lagos, Abuja, Kano and Port Harcourt via Open-Meteo API.
⚽
Man City Match Tracker
Shows the last result and next fixture with a live countdown timer. Updates automatically from city.json in the repo — edited manually after each match in about 30 seconds.
💰
2026 Wealth Path Simulator
Enter your monthly deposit, current balance, a fintech strategy (Neobank 15%, Locked Savings 12%, Traditional Bank 4%) and a target month. Uses compound interest to project your balance, interest earned and portfolio breakdown. Auto-recalculates on any input change.
⚙
Fee Calculator (My Tools → Tab 1)
Independent Nigerian payment gateway fee calculator. Compare Flutterwave, Paystack, Monnify and Squad. Includes reverse calc, bulk invoicing, live FX rates, donut chart and PDF receipt export.
🎮
Squaredodger (My Tools → Tab 2)
A browser arcade game — dodge the red squares for as long as you can. Built in vanilla JS with skins, a leaderboard and Web Audio API sound effects.
📊
Fintech 2026 (My Tools → Tab 3)
An interactive intelligence report on Nigeria's fintech ecosystem — company cards, funding timeline, valuation charts, M&A acquisition map, and six DECODE insight pieces. Built as a DECODE content project.
🎬
What to Watch Tonight (My Tools → Tab 4)
AI-powered movie and series recommender. Pick your mood, select genres (Nollywood, anime, K-Drama, Hollywood and more), add context like "something like Money Heist but African" and AI returns personalised picks with a reason why you'll love each one.
💹
TrueProfit Calculator (My Tools → Tab 5)
Side hustle profit calculator for Nigerian business owners and entrepreneurs. Enter your revenue, COGS, labour, and tick hidden expenses (delivery, data, packaging, platform fees and more). Calculates your real net profit, margin, ROI, and profit per unit — with a smart insight on what it means for your business.
📰
Daily Digest (My Tools → Tab 6)
Live Nigerian news headlines aggregated from Punch, Vanguard, TechCabal, Premium Times, Channels TV and Daily Post. Filter by Politics, Business, Tech, Sports or Entertainment. Updates every 30 minutes automatically — no app switching needed.
⏳
Naira Time Machine (My Tools → Tab 7)
Enter any amount and pick a past year — see exactly how many bags of rice, litres of petrol, and dollars that money could buy then vs now in 2026. Visualises Nigeria's purchasing power erosion since 2005 using hardcoded historical price data from NBS, CBN and Vanguard.
Built with HTML, CSS and vanilla JS · Deployed on Vercel · All data sources are free and open
